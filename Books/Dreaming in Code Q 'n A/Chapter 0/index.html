Chapter 0

  1. Who wrote “software is hard?” Who is that guy?
    2. Donald Knuth, author of The Art of Computer Programing, wrote “software is hard.”

  2. Programmers start counting at what number?
    3. Programmers (and computers) count from zero.

  3. What was the original sense of a “hacker?”
    4. An “obsessive programming tinkerer” was the original characterization towards hackers.

  4. According to a 2002 NIST study what % of software came in significantly late, over budget, or was canceled?
    5. 2/3 of projects were late, over budget, or cancelled according to the study.

  5. Who wrote the 1987 essay entitled “No Silver Bullet?”
    6. Frederick P. Brooks Jr. wrote “No Silver Bullet”
